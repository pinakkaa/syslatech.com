About Star and Sky Spa

Star and Sky Spa is a trusted and professional spa located in Katpadi, Vellore, offering high-quality wellness and relaxation therapies. With over 10 years of experience, we are known as one of the most reliable and professional spas in Vellore, providing services delivered by well-trained and certified therapists in a clean, calm, and welcoming environment.
